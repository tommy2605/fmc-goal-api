The Fulfilment of The Outpouring of The Holy Spirit

INTRODUCTION

After Jesus rose from the dead and when He was about to ascent to Heaven, He told His disciples not to leave Jerusalem and wait for the Father’s promise.  The Father’s promise is the baptism of the Holy Spirit (Acts 1:4-5, Acts 2:1-4,38-39).

CONTENT

Have fellowship

  1. Fellowship with the Lord Jesus. Acts 1:4 “And being assembled together with them…”

    From this verse we can draw a lesson that in order to receive the Father’s promise we must have fellowship with Jesus (John 14:26).

    • Through Jesus’ sacrifice on the cross we who were far from the Father will be able to return and have unity with the Father (Ephesians 2:12-16, John 14:6).
    • The price for that communion is very expensive, the price that Christ must be humbled and crucified. So we need to maintain it (1 Petrus 1:18-19).
  2. The Lord blesses and cares about fellowship (Matthew 18:18-20)
    • This verse is confirmed by Psalms 133:1-3, that the Fellowship of believers in the Lord pleases God and He wants to be in the midst of it.
    • A Fellowship in the Lord flows the anointing oil, it is a picture of the Holy Spirit that refreshes us and brings blessings to us, His people (Isaiah 44:3).

CONCLUSION

Stay in the fellowship with the Lord and with other believers and the Holy Spirit will flow and pour upon us.
